PET ANIMALS

What are pet animals?

Pet animals are friendly animals that need love and affection from humans. They are the best friends of human beings. Pet animals are beautiful animals that need shelter for them to be protected. Dogs, cats, rabbits, and birds are the most popular pet animals.

WILD ANIMALS

What are wild animals?

Wild animals are animals that live in forests. These animals are not normally domesticated.These animals live on their own without any help from people, a wild animal finds its own shelter, food, water and all other needs.
